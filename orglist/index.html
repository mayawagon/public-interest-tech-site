    About

    The Technology and Social Justice field scan is intended to find out who works with technology for social justice and/or the public interest, how people get into the work, and what needs to be done to support it. Research Action Design (RAD) and the Open Technology Institute (OTI) are conducting a community-led field scan of public interest technology that centers diverse practitioners and identifies challenges and opportunities. The field scan will identify people, groups, institutions, and networks who are already working on public interest technology, and explore how best to foster the growth of the field. This is a participatory research project that will be guided by a group of practitioners who are deeply engaged in the work, from diverse perspectives.
